# StudyVault

## Tagline

Your Academic Knowledge Hub.

## Elevator Pitch

StudyVault is a modern academic knowledge management platform built specifically for university students.

Instead of simply storing files like Google Drive, StudyVault organizes lecture materials by semester, course, and category while providing powerful search, preview, and AI-assisted learning features.

## Vision

Become the best academic workspace for students.

## Mission

Help students organize, revisit, and understand every lecture material throughout their academic journey.

## Target Users

Primary
- University Students

Secondary
- Teaching Assistants
- Tutors
- Student Organizations

## Problems

Students receive hundreds of lecture files.

These files are scattered across:

- Google Drive
- Downloads
- WhatsApp
- Telegram
- LMS
- Email

After several semesters, finding one lecture module becomes frustrating.

StudyVault solves this problem.
